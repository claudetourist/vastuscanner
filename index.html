What this does

Find true north for your front door

This pins the exact direction your main entrance faces — the one number every Vastu reading hangs on. It checks two ways and compares them, so you can trust the result:

  1. Your phone's compass, read right at the doorway.
  2. A satellite view, where you point an arrow the way your door faces.

Before you start

Stand at your main entrance. Step just outside if you can — metal gates, the fridge, and wiring confuse phone compasses, and a few steps out gives a cleaner reading.

You'll be asked to allow motion and location access. Both stay on your phone; nothing is sent anywhere.

Works fully offline once loaded. Needs no account.
